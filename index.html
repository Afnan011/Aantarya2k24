RULES

  • Maximum number of participants from one department is 15.
  • The fest is open to all BCA, BSC, MCA, and MSC-Computer Science students.
  • Teams have to register through our website.
  • The registration fee is ₹1,500 per team.
  • All the participants must be present before 9.00 AM.
  • A Team must participate in all the events to be in the contention for the Overall Championship.
  • IT Manager, Photography, coding and web designing participants should not participate in any other events.
  • Participants are requested to produce their college ID on the Fest day.
  • All participants are required to be available on campus for both days of the event.
  • The department/convener reserves the right to take action in case of any misconduct.
  • If accommodation is required, it should be mentioned at the time of registration.
  • The decisions of the judges will be final and binding by all regards.
  • If any issues regarding the payment of registration fees, please contact the Committee members.
  • There will be a cash prize and trophy awarded to the overall champions as well as the runners.
